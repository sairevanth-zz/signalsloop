## 4. The Workflow
**Headline:** From Signal to Success.
1.  **Listen:** Hunters sweep the web and your internal channels (Slack, CRM) for signals.
2.  **Synthesize:** Agents cluster feedback into themes and actionable insights.
3.  **Plan:** The Spec Writer drafts the solution; The Devil's Advocate stress-tests it.
4.  **Build:** Syncs with your issue tracker (Jira/Linear) for execution.
5.  **Measure:** Tracks the actual impact on your KPIs.
